 Cyber Safety Hub

                        
Quick tips, guides, and lessons to help you spot scams and stay secure online.

                    


                    

                        

                            
WARNING SIGNAL

                            
How to Spot Fake Internships

                            
Fake job offers often require advance fee deposits for "recruitment software", "laptop shipping", or "certification modules". No real employer will charge you to start working.

                            
Golden Rule: Never send money to get a job or internship.

                        


                        

                            
DOMAIN VERIFY

                            
How to Avoid Scholarship Scams

                            
Check the domain spelling of official listservs. Scammers will mimic brands (like using @gmail.com or @scholarship-govt.cc) instead of governmental/academic .edu or .gov handles.

                            
Golden Rule: Always check official email handles.

                        


                        

                            
CHAT WARNING

                            
Safe Online Habits

                            
Avoid recruiters who demand that all communication, interviews, and contracts be carried out solely on Telegram or WhatsApp chat channels without official portal links.

                            
Golden Rule: Request corporate links or formal verification details.
